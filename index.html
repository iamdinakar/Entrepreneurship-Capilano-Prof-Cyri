How I design this lifestyle

Operating principles

A few simple rules that keep the business aligned with the life I actually want, not the life the internet says I should chase.

  • Lifestyle first, business second. Income should support the life I want, not consume it.
  • Fewer things, done deeply. Keep the portfolio light so each project gets real attention.
  • AI as a collaborator, not a replacement. Use it to explore, sketch, and test—not to erase my own voice.
  • Simple over impressive. If a process can be simpler, cleaner, or calmer, it should be.
  • Protect unstructured time. Space to think and play is a non-negotiable part of the system.
