How I build

Operating principles

Simple rules that guide how I choose projects, work with people, and make decisions when the path isn’t obvious.

  • Build for clarity, not complexity. Every project should be explainable in one sentence.
  • Ship small, real things often. Momentum beats perfection; feedback is more accurate than theory.
  • Respect energy & attention. Align work with strengths and protect focus from busywork.
  • Relationships > transactions. Play the long game. Be easy to recommend, not easy to forget.
  • Systems over willpower. Design processes that make the right things happen by default.
